# Build Log

## Step-by-Step Build Process

### Step 1: Preparation
1. Gather all necessary components: CPU, GPU, RAM, Motherboard, Power Supply, SSD/HDD, etc.
2. Prepare your workspace with anti-static measures. 

### Step 2: Install the CPU
1. Open the CPU socket on the motherboard.
2. Align the CPU with the socket (notch and arrows should align).
3. Carefully place the CPU into the socket and secure it.

### Step 3: Install the RAM
1. Open the RAM slots on the motherboard.
2. Align the RAM stick with the slot, ensuring the notch aligns with the slot.
3. Press down firmly until the clips on either side click into place.

### Step 4: Install the GPU
1. Identify the PCIe slot on the motherboard.
2. Remove any necessary case covers for the GPU.
3. Align the GPU with the PCIe slot and press down firmly until it clicks into place.
4. Secure the GPU to the case with screws.

### Step 5: Install Storage
1. Secure SSD/HDD into its slot or bay in the case.
2. Connect the SATA power and data cables from the motherboard to the storage devices.

### Step 6: Connect Power Supply
1. Place the power supply in the designated area of the case.
2. Connect all necessary cables from the PSU to the motherboard, GPU and peripherals.

### Step 7: Cable Management
1. Organize all cables to ensure good airflow within the case.

### Step 8: Final Check
1. Ensure all components are securely attached.
2. Check for loose cables or screws.

### Step 9: Power On
1. Connect the power cable to the PSU.
2. Power on the system and enter BIOS/UEFI to confirm component recognition.

### Step 10: Install Operating System
1. Follow the instructions to install your preferred OS.
